What is BIX.fi?

BIX.fi is a digital asset platform built around the idea that crypto should be easier to use in daily life. It combines a self-custody wallet experience with card-based spending, stablecoin collateral, cross-chain swaps, and mobile payment convenience.

A simple bridge between crypto and daily spending

The main idea behind BIX.fi is practical: digital assets should not stay locked inside complicated crypto tools. The platform positions itself as a way to connect wallet control, card usage, and payment flexibility in one clean experience.

According to BIX.fi, the platform supports card usage, Apple Pay and Google Pay integration, cross-chain swaps, configurable monthly spending limits, and stablecoin collateral across multiple chains.
